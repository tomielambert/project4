Downfall Timeline

In this timeline Link fails to defeat Ganondorf which allows him to obtain all three pieces of the Triforce. These events lead to:

  • A Link to the Past
  • A Link Between Worlds
  • Tri Force Heroes
  • Oracle of Ages
  • Oracle of Seasons
  • Link's Awakening
  • The Legend of Zelda
